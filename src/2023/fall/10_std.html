Week 1 of 3-week Pre-test Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • Pre-test mesocycle is lower average volume and high intensity.
  • There will be fewer technical exercises and accessories, but more focus on the classical lifts
  • Last workout of the cycle will be a test event (set PR's / determine maxes)
Athlete Notes
  • The pre-test mesocycle is designed to peak your performance on the last day of the cycle, which will be used to PR / determine your maxes.
  • Treat every rep like a competition attempt.
  • We are not trying to make you bigger and stronger at this point. The next few weeks is about allowing your gains to be realized.
  • All squats: regardless of the intensity, recover as quickly as possible (focus on speed-strength).
  • If you miss Sunday's workout, do not try to make it up unless you can do it on Monday. Alternatively, substitute the following for hang snatches and back jerks on Tuesday:
    • Snatch 65x3 70(2x3) 75x3 80x3 83(2x3)
    • C&J 65x3 70(2x3) 75x3 80x3 83x3

Sunday

  1. Snatch 60x3 65x3 70x3 75x3 80x3 83(2x3)

  2. C&J 60x3 65x3 70x3 75x3 80x3 83(2x3)

  3. Back Squat 60x5 65x5 70x4 75x3 80x3 83(2x3)

  4. Pause Snatch Pull (1mm off the floor 10 secs, just below the knee 10 secs, just above the knee 10secs) 55x2 60x2 65x2 70x2

  5. Weighted Planks 3x1 minute

Tuesday

  1. Hang Snatch Below Knee - 3 secs pause in hang 60x3 65(2x3) 70x3 75(3x3)

    • Keep your arms passive / relaxed during the (dead) hang.
    • When initiating the movement from the hang, lead by pushing with your legs rather than pulling with your lower back.

  2. Back Jerk 60x3 65(2x3) 70x3 75(2x3)

  3. Back Squat 60x4 65x4 70x4 75x3 80x3

  4. Weighted Planks 3x1 minute

  5. Side Planks 3x1 minute

  6. Wrist Curls 4x15-18

Thursday - Maxout

  1. Snatch 65x3 70x3 75x2 80x2 85x2 90x2 95x1 100x1 105x1

  2. C&J 65x3 70x3 75x2 80x2 85x2 90x2 95x1 100x1 105x1

  3. Rest & relax. Sunday will be fairly heavy, but not a maxout.
