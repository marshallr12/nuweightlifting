Week 3 of 3-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

Competing

Coaches' Notes
  • Full competition deload.
  • Volume and intensity taper for the meet.
  • Athletes should still base intensities off of their PR's at the start of the cycle.
  • For Sunday's workout: first time competitors often fail to wait for the down signal. It's useful on their heaviest lifts to have someone sitting in a chair directly in their line of sight who acts like a referee. On their heavy singles, the ref should give them a down signal. If the athlete puts the weight down before being given the signal, the ref should yell "No lift!". Also, the down signal should only be given after the athlete is motionless for 3 seconds.
Athlete Notes
  • Treat Sunday like a competition modeling day. Wear your singlet.
  • Pretend every lift this week is an attempt at the competition.
  • At the end of every workout this week (time permitting) add technique shadowing:
    • Snatch bar(5x3)
    • C&J bar(5x3)
  • Avoid adding extra exercises and heavier attempts because you happen to feel strong or have extra energy. That means the deload is working correctly, so save it for the meet.
  • Seet you at the meet!
