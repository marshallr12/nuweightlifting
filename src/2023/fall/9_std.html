Week 3 of Mesocycle 3

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • Last week of preparatory mesocycle 2
  • Total volume is very low, but consolidated into 3 days.
Athlete Notes
  • This is the last week of the preparatory mesocycles.
  • When you get back from vacation, you will start the 3-week pre-competition mesocycle. The average volume will be reduced and average intensity will be higher.
  • Heavy days will be Sundays, since the last session of the Macrocycle will be a Sunday maxout / PR attempts.
  • Since your heavy day is early this week (Tuesday), you might feel more fatigue (and the weights heavier) than usual. Try to maintain your technique regardless.

Sunday

  1. Halting Snatch Deadlift (at hips) 3 secs + High Pull + PS 65x1 70(2x1) 75x1 80x1 83(2x1)

    • Make sure in the halting position, you feel your center of gravity balanced over the middle to balls of your feet, and the bar plumb beneath the rear of your armpits.
    • On the high pull, try to find the same halting configuration when the bar contacts your hips (but don't pause - keep the bar moving!)
    • On the power snatch, try to reproduce the same movement as the high pull (but don't stop - keep moving the bar up and dropping under it until it is fixated overhead!)

  2. PC + PJ + Back Power Jerk 60x3 65x3 70x3 75x2 80x2 83x1

    • Move your elbows as wide and high as possible before turning them over quickly in the clean
    • In the jerk, start with your feet at a width that is ideal for jumping, and catch the weight with your feet at a width that is ideal for a deep squat.

  3. Back Squat 60x5 65x5 70x4 75x3 80x3 83(3x2)

  4. Snatch Pull 90(2x3) 95(2x3)

  5. Side Planks 3x1 minute

  6. 4 rounds:

    • Calf raises x15-18
    • Wrist Curls x15-18

Tuesday

  1. Snatch 65x3 70(2x3) 75x3 80x3 85x2 90x2 + a couple heavier singles

  2. C&J 65x3 70(2x3) 75x3 80x2 85x2 90x2 95x1 + a couple heavier singles. Only increase the weight after 95 if the previous rep was technically sound and you'd be willing to bet $20 that you'll make the attempt.

  3. Back Squat 65x4 70x3 75x3 80x3 85x2 90x1 95x1 100x1

  4. Abalakov Jump 4x10

  5. Weighted Planks 3x1 minute

  4. Front Squat 60x5 70x4 80x4
