Week 3 of Mesocycle 3

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • Last week of preparatory mesocycle 2
  • Total volume is very low, but consolidated into 3 days.
Athlete Notes
  • This is the last week of the preparatory mesocycles.
  • When you get back from vacation, you will start the 3-week pre-competition mesocycle. The average volume will be reduced and average intensity will be higher.
  • Heavy days will be Sundays instead of Thursdays so that your body will adjust to lifting on a Sunday meet.
  • Try to make it back to school in time for next Sunday's training after vacation.
  • The ideal day to complete workout 3 is Friday. If you complete this workout, training on the Sunday after vacation will be more successful.
  • Since your heavy day is early this week (Tuesday), you might feel more fatigue (and the weights heavier) than usual. Try to maintain your technique regardless.
