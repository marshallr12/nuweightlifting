Week 3 of 3-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

Not Competing

Coaches' Notes
  • Full deload.
  • Volume and intensity taper for the meet.
  • Athletes should still base intensities off of their PR's at the start of the cycle.
  • For Thursday's workout: first time competitors often fail to wait for the down signal. It's useful on their heaviest lifts to have someone sitting in a chair directly in their line of sight who acts like a referee. On their heavy singles, the ref should give them a down signal. If the athlete puts the weight down before being given the signal, the ref should yell "No lift!". Also, the down signal should only be given after the athlete is motionless for 3 seconds.
Athlete Notes
  • Treat Thursday like a competition modeling day. Wear your singlet if you have one.
  • Pretend every lift this week is an attempt at a competition.
  • Avoid adding extra exercises and heavier attempts because you happen to feel strong or have extra energy. That means the deload is working correctly, so save it for your maxout day.
  • Good luck on Thursday!
