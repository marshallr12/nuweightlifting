Sunday 3/10

This workout is meant to be easy / light conditioning after Spring Break. Keep perceived effort around 50-70%.

  1. Snatch Press BN + Drop Snatch + Overhead squat 5x1

  2. Muscle snatch 5x3

  3. Muscle clean + push press hold 5 secs 5x3 not too heavy

    In the finish of the press, hold the press overhead while balanced with your heels 1 mm (barely touching) the floor. Think of the finish as holding a stretch to the sky.

  4. Front squat triples to about 70%, then starting at your last front squat weight, back squat triples to about 70%.

  5. Planks + side planks + reverse planks 3x30 secs
