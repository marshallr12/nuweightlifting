Week 1 of 4-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • Short 8-week macrocycle (Two 4-week mesocycles) culminating in a maxout
Athlete Notes
  • Very high volume but low intensity week
  • Reconditioning & reintroduction of training stimulus after a long break.
  • Tuesday is a long workout.

Tuesday 1/9

  1. Tempo Snatch bar(5x3)

  2. Snatch Pull to below knee + SP to above knee + SP to hips + Snatch HP + PS 50x1 55x1 60x1 63x1 66x1 69x1

    Each segment of the movement should start from the floor with a light touch

  3. Clean Pull to below knee + CP to above knee + CP to upper thigh + Clean HP + PC 50x1 55x1 60x1 63x1 66x1 69x1

    Each segment of the movement should start from the floor with a light touch

  4. Back Squat 55x10 60x5 65(2x4) 70x3 75x3 80(2x3)

  5. Clean Grip BN Press 3x10

  6. Planks 3x1 minute
