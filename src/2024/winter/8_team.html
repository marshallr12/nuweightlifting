Week 4 of 4-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • Full competition deload
  • It's important for the athletes to stick to the prescribed volume and intensity, or the deload will not go well.
  • Last week of the 8-week macrocycle
Athlete Notes
  • Count out how many days you are from your competition session, then do the appropriate workout. If you are competing Thursday, you will have already performed day -4 on Sunday.
  • Skip (do not make up) training sessions if you can't train a particular day relative to your competition day. It is more important to lift at the correct intensity relative to your competition day.
  • Each day, add Snatch bar(5x3) + C&J bar(5x3) at the end of the workout.

-4 days out from Competition Day

  1. lifts up to 75% in snatch and c&j (15-18 reps)

  2. Back Squat 75x2 80x2 85x2

  3. Planks 3x1 minute

-3 days out

  1. lifts up to 70% in snatch and c&j (15-18 reps)

  2. Front Squat 80(3x2)

  3. Abalakov Jump 3x8

  4. Reverse Planks 3x1 minute

-2 days out

  1. lifts up to 65% in snatch and c&J (15-18 reps)

  2. Back Squat 80(3x2)

  3. Side Planks 3x1 minute

-1 day out

  1. lifts up to 55% in snatch and c&J (15 reps)

  2. Back Squats 80(3x1)

  3. Abalakov Jump 1x5
