Week 1 of 4-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • Start of the pre-competition phase.
  • Average volume drops and average intensity increases significantly.
Athlete Notes
  • In the pre-competition mesocycle (4-weeks prior to meet day), the focus is no longer on building work capacity and strength, but rather, allowing your acquired power and technique to express itself.
  • There will be more focus on the classical movements, including heavier attempts.

Tuesday

  1. Power Snatch 60(2x3) 65x3 70x3 75x3 80(2x3)

  2. Tall Snatch + Overhead Squat + Drop Snatch 5x1

  3. PC+PJ 65x4 70x3 75x3 80(2x3)

  4. Back Squat 65x5 70x4 75x3 80x3 83x3

  5. Snatch Pull 85(2x3) 90(2x3)

  6. Weighted Planks 3x1 minute

Thursday

  1. Power Snatch + Snatch Balance + Snatch 60x1 65x1 70x1 75x1 80x1

    On the snatch balance, minimize the boost / cheat to get under. If you can perform a drop snatch, that is best.

  2. C&J 60x3 65x3 70x3 75(2x3) 80x3

  3. Back Squat 75x3 80x3 85x3

  4. 4 rounds:

    • Finger wrist curls x15-18 (let your fingers roll open at the bottom of the movement)
    • Abalakov Jump x10

  5. Weighted Planks 3x1 minute

Sunday

  1. Snatch 60x3 65(2x3) 70x3 75x3 80x2 87x1 95x1 95+ x1

  2. C&J 60x3 65(2x3) 70x3 75x3 80(2x3) 87x1 95x1 95+ x1

  3. Back Squat 60x7 65x5 70x3 75x2 80x2 85x2 90x1 95x1 95+ x1
