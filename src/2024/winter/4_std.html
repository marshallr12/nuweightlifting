Week 4 of 4-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • Low volume (deload)
Athlete Notes
  • Lower volume this week, but some heavy lifts on Sunday.

Tuesday

  1. Tempo Snatch bar(5x3)

  2. Power Snatch + Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat 60x1 65x1 70(2x1) 75x1 80x1 83x1

  3. PC+2PJ 60x2 65x2 70x2 75(2x2) 80x2 83x2

  4. Back Squat 60x5 65x5 70x5 75x3 80x3 83x3

  5. Clean Pull 90(2x3) 95(2x3)

  6. 4 rounds:

    • Wrist curls x15-18
    • Abalakov Jump x10
    • Pullups x10

  7. Weighted Planks 3x1 minute

Thursday

  1. Tall Snatch bar(4x3)

  2. Snatch 65x3 70x3 75x3 80x3 83x3

    Try to reproduce the feeling and timing of the tall snatch

  3. Tall Clean bar(4x3)

  4. C&J 60x3 65(2x3) 70x3 75x3 80x3

    Try to reproduce the feeling and timing of the tall clean

  5. Zombie Front Squat 10 secs descent 55x1 60x1 65x1 70x1 75x1 80x1 83x2

  6. Snatch Pull 90(2x3) 95(2x3)

  7. 3 rounds:

    • Side Plank Banded Clamshell x10
    • Abalakov Jump x10

  8. Pinch Grip Plate hold 3x1 minute

Sunday

  1. Snatch 65x3 70(2x3) 75x3 80x3 85x2 90x1 92x1

  2. C&J 60x3 65x3 70x3 75x3 80x3 85x2 90x1 92x1 optional 95x1

  3. Back Squat 65x5 70x4 75x4 80x3 85x3 90x3 92x1 95x1

  4. 4 rounds:

    • Pullups x10
    • Triceps extensions x10

  5. Weighted Planks 3x1 minute
