Week 3 of 4-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes

Athlete Notes
  • Tuesday's workout is long and tiring.
  • Sunday's workout includes some heavy lifts.

Tuesday

  1. Drop Snatch 5x3

    "Drop Snatch" is a variation of the snatch balance where you start with your heels 1 inch off the ground, bar resting on your back and drop into catch position without lifting the bar *any* higher than where it was in your start position. The idea is to free fall until your hamstrings land on your calves without first boosting the bar up to give yourself more time to hit bottom.

  2. Tall Snatch 5x3

    On the tall snatch, avoid lifting your arms up significantly before dropping down. Ideally, lead by breaking at the knees (to go down) and simultaneously lifting up your elbows. If the weight is too heavy, you will also instinctively lift the bar up quite a bit before breaking at the knees, so choose a weight that is light enough for you to jump down without first lifting the bar up.
