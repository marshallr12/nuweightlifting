Week 3 of 4-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • The full deload for competition day begins this week, depending on which day the athlete is competing.
  • It's important for the athletes to stick to the prescribed volume and intensity, or the deload will not go well.
Athlete Notes
  • Ideally, your last 90%+ training session should occurs 7 days out from your competition day.
  • If your competition day is Thursday, February 28th or Friday, February 29th, swap Sunday's workout with Thursday's workout.
  • Do not lift any heavier or more sets than is programmed, even if you feel like you're not getting much of a workout or feel like you can lift much more. That is the point of a deload - save it for the meet.
