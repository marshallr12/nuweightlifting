Week 2 of 4-week Pre-competition Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes
  • Please send feedback on the length of the workouts (too long, too short, etc.). Workouts should be a little shorter this week compared to last week.
Athlete Notes
  • Lower volume this week (minor deload)

Tuesday

  1. Snatch Pull to above knee + 5 secs hold + Snatch Pull to hips + 5 secs hold + PS from floor 60(2x1) 65(2x1) 70(2x1) 75x1

    Make sure your center of gravity stays in the ball of foot to mid foot during the hold (avoid shifting into your heels, i.e., counter-balancing). Use your toes (especially big toe) actively to maintain your forward center of gravity and avoid being pulled over.

  2. Clean Pull to below knee + 5 secs hold + Clean Pull to Upper thigh + 5 secs hold + PC + PJ + Back PJ + Back Jerk 60x1 65x1 70(2x1) 75x1 80(2x1)

    Make sure your center of gravity stays in the ball of foot to mid foot during the hold (avoid shifting into your heels, i.e., counter-balancing). Use your toes (especially big toe) actively to maintain your forward center of gravity and avoid being pulled over.

  3. Back Squat 60(2x6) 65(2x5) 70x4 75x3 80(2x3)

  4. Snatch Pull 85(2x3) 90(2x3)

  5. 4 rounds:

    • Plank Hop x10
    • Pullups x10
    • Wrist curls x15-18

Thursday

  1. Snatch Balance 60x4 65x3 70x3 75x3 80x3

    At the lightest weights, perform a drop snatch. Add a slight heave up to the bar as necessary when the weight gets too heavy to perform a pure drop snatch. Avoid performing a snatch-grip back PJ + overhead squat. The goal is to complete the movement by moving your body down rather than relying on heaving the bar up.

  2. Clean + PJ + Back Jerk 60x2 65(2x2) 70x2 75x2

  3. Front Squat 60x2 65x2 70x2 75x2 80x2 85x2

  4. 4 rounds:

    • Side Plank Banded Clamshell x10
    • Barbell Rows x10
    • Single leg calf raise x15-18
    • Paloff Press x10

  5. Prone cobra 3x1 minute

Sunday

  1. Snatch 60x4 65(2x3) 70x3 75x3 80x3 85x2

  2. C&J 55x4 60x3 65x3 70x3 75x3 80x3 85x2

  3. Back Squat 60x5 65x5 70x5 75x4 80x4 85x3 90(2x2)

  4. 4 rounds:

    • Abalakov jump x10
    • Dumbbell Pullover x10

  5. 3 rounds:

    • Pinch Grip Plate Hold x1 minute or to failure
    • Weighted Planks 3x1 minute
