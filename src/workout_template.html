Week 1 of 3-week Conditioning Mesocycle

General Program

Coaches' Notes
Athlete Notes

Sunday

  1. Tempo Zombie Air Squat (10/10/10) body(4x2). 30 secs per rep x 2 reps x 4 sets = 8 mins + 30 secs rest per set = 10 mins

    • Back should remain flat and chest open, rather than rounded with shoulders folded inwards.

Tuesday

  1. Clean Start Position + Hold 10 secs 5x3

    • It is important to practice approaching the bar and finding your start position. On each rep, step away from the bar, and repeat the process of trying to find your start position.
    • The particulars of the start position such as grip width and torso angle will vary according to your anatomy.
    • Key points for the start position regardless of anatomy:
      • Your back must be rigid, and either flat or slightly arched (never rounded).

Thursday

  1. Drop Squat body(5x4)

Day 4

Monday, Wednesday, Friday, or Saturday

  1. Snatch Grip Upright row 4x8-10

    • Keep the chest open and back arched / rigid.
    • Think of lifting your elbows directly to the ceiling, rather than using your hands to lift the bar up.
    • If your elbows begin to go backwards, you are lifting the bar too high.
